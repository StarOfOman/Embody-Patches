Outfit selection (OF_) - POST
OF_{clothingValue} - Equips a clothing item to its slot
  5 slots: head, torso, legs, feet, back
  Items are character-specific (Lucy, Sarah, Harry, Zach)
  Examples: OF_Tops_Lucy.TurtleNeckSweater, OF_Bottoms_Harry.CargoPant, OF_Shoes_Zach.Sneakers

Head slot:
  Headwear_None

Torso slot:
  SleevelessDress - Tops_Lucy.SleevelessDress, Tops_Sarah.SleevelessDress
  TurtleNeckSweater - Tops_Lucy.TurtleNeckSweater, Tops_Sarah.TurtleNeckSweater, Tops_Harry.TurtleNeckSweater
  TShirt - Tops_Lucy.TShirt, Tops_Sarah.TShirt, Tops_Harry.TShirt, Tops_Zach.TShirt
  TripleLayerVest - Tops_Lucy.TripleLayerVest, Tops_Sarah.TripleLayerVest, Tops_Harry.TripleLayerVest, Tops_Zach.TripleLayerVest
  EmbodyPuffer - Tops_Lucy.EmbodyPuffer (Lucy only)
  LivepeerOutfit - Tops_Lucy.LivepeerOutfit (Lucy only)
  PumpkinWitch - Tops_Lucy.PumpkinWitch (Lucy only)

Legs slot:
  Bottoms_None
  Skirt - Bottoms_Lucy.Skirt, Bottoms_Sarah.Skirt
  CargoPant - Bottoms_Lucy.CargoPant, Bottoms_Sarah.CargoPant, Bottoms_Harry.CargoPant, Bottoms_Zach.CargoPant
  Shorts - Bottoms_Lucy.Shorts, Bottoms_Sarah.Shorts, Bottoms_Harry.Shorts, Bottoms_Zach.Shorts
  AthleticShortsF - Bottoms_Lucy.AthleticShortsF, Bottoms_Sarah.AthleticShortsF

Feet slot:
  Shoes_None
  Sneakers - Shoes_Lucy.Sneakers, Shoes_Sarah.Sneakers, Shoes_Harry.Sneakers, Shoes_Zach.Sneakers
  Heels - Shoes_Lucy.Heels, Shoes_Sarah.Heels

Back slot:
  Magic_None
  Magic_Wings

Auto-pairing: selecting a top automatically sets matching bottoms:
  SleevelessDress -> Bottoms_None (dress covers legs)
  TurtleNeckSweater -> Skirt
  TShirt -> CargoPant
  TripleLayerVest -> Shorts

Clothing skins (OFC_) - POST
OFC_{category}_{type}.{texture} - Applies a texture skin to the current clothing
  Only supported on: SleevelessDress (20 skins), TShirt (16 skins), CargoPant (16 skins), Skirt (5 skins)
  Examples: OFC_Tops_SleevelessDress.Phoenix, OFC_Bottoms_CargoPant.PinkCamo

SleevelessDress skins (20):
  Default, BeigeWoven, BlueClouds, BWFerro, BWShred, BWStudded, CarbonFiber, GreenGrid, GreenNet, GreenSquared, GreyFeathers, GreyFelt, LinenBeige, Phoenix, Polkadot, RedNylon, RipstopFloral, RoyalGreen, RoyalPink, SalmonCotton

TShirt skins (16):
  Default, BeigeWash, NavyWhiteStripe, PinkCamo, PinkHexLogo, Starlight, ShadedSquare, RWFlannel, GreyLogo, Grey, GreenStripeLogo, GreenStripe, BlueDiamond, BlackLogo, PlainWhiteLogo, PlainWhite

CargoPant skins (16):
  Default, AfghanCamo, BWCamo, BWHex, CaddyFlannel, Dart, FullCamo, PinkCamo, PinkHearts, PinkPlad, RBFlannel, RWFlannel, WHex, WhiteoutHex, WOHex, YellowThread

Skirt skins (5):
  Default, Tiger, NavyLint, BlueFloral, RedFloral

Custom UV map textures (OFCD_) - POST
OFCD_{objectType}_{url} - Applies a custom UV map texture from a public URL to clothing
  Currently supported: OFCD_Legs_{url} (Skirt UV map)
  URL must be publicly accessible (HTTPS, e.g., ImgBB hosted)
  Created via AI generation (Stability AI / Gemini) or freehand doodle on the Custom Materials page
  Example: OFCD_Legs_https://i.ibb.co/example/custom-skirt-texture.png
