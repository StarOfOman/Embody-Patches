Hairstyle selection - POST
HS_{Character}.{Style} - Selects a hairstyle (32 styles per character)
  Characters: Lucy, Harry, Sarah, Zach
  Styles: Updo, UpdoBuns, UpdoBraids, LowPonytail, BobSlick, BobMessy, BobStraight, BobBangs, BobLayered, LongStraightBangs, LongStraight, LongMessy, AfroLong, AfroFade, FauxMohawk, Mohawk, Layered, SideSweptFringe, Waves, Buzzcut, Casual, Clean, CoilBuzz, Cornrows, CurlyFade, HairLoss, ShortMessy, Pixie, PulledBack, Recede, SlickBack, SweptUp
  Examples: HS_Lucy.Updo, HS_Harry.TwistedBraids, HS_Sarah.LongStraight, HS_Zach.MessyShort

Base hair color (HCR/HCG/HCB) - POST
HCR.{value} - Red channel (0.00-1.00)
HCG.{value} - Green channel (0.00-1.00)
HCB.{value} - Blue channel (0.00-1.00)

Float sliders (HAIRF) - POST
HAIRF_hairMelanin_{value} - Melanin amount (darker pigment)
HAIRF_hairRedness_{value} - Red pigment amount
HAIRF_HairRoughness_{value} - Surface roughness
HAIRF_Desat_{value} - Desaturation amount
HAIRF_Highlights_{value} - Highlights intensity on/off
HAIRF_HighlightsBlending_{value} - Highlights blend smoothness
HAIRF_HighlightsIntensity_{value} - Highlights strength
HAIRF_HighlightsMelanin_{value} - Highlights melanin
HAIRF_HighlightsRedness_{value} - Highlights red pigment
HAIRF_Ombre_{value} - Ombre effect intensity on/off
HAIRF_OmbreContrast_{value} - Ombre contrast between root and tip
HAIRF_OmbreIntensity_{value} - Ombre strength
HAIRF_OmbreMelanin_{value} - Ombre melanin
HAIRF_OmbreRedness_{value} - Ombre red pigment
HAIRF_OpacityNear_{value} - Hair opacity up close
HAIRF_OpacityFar_{value} - Hair opacity at distance
HAIRF_Spec0_{value} - Primary specular highlight
HAIRF_Spec1_{value} - Secondary specular highlight
HAIRF_Scraggle_{value} - Hair scraggle/flyaway amount

Color pickers (HAIRC) - POST
HAIRC_HighlightshairDye_{r}_{g}_{b} - Highlights dye color
HAIRC_OmbrehairDye_{r}_{g}_{b} - Ombre dye color

All color values are RGB floats 0.00-1.00.
Presets (Brunette, Blonde, Redhead, etc.) send all float and color commands at once.
