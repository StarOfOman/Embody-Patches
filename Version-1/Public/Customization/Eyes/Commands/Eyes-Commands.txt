All eye parameters use format EYE_{param}_{value} - POST
Slider UI range -100 to 200, value divided by 100 before sending (float range -1.00 to 2.00)

Pupil & Iris Shape (20 params) - POST
EYE_CloudyIrisHardness_{value} - Cloudy iris edge hardness
EYE_CloudyIrisRadius_{value} - Cloudy iris radius
EYE_DistortPanX_{value} - Iris distortion pan X
EYE_DistortPanY_{value} - Iris distortion pan Y
EYE_DistortScale_{value} - Iris distortion scale
EYE_DistortStrength_{value} - Iris distortion strength
EYE_Iris Concavity Power_{value} - Iris concavity power
EYE_Iris Concavity Scale_{value} - Iris concavity scale
EYE_IrisBleedWidth_{value} - Iris bleed width at edges
EYE_IrisBorderWidth_{value} - Iris border width
EYE_IrisBrightness_{value} - Iris brightness
EYE_IrisMaskWidth_{value} - Iris mask width
EYE_IrisRoughness_{value} - Iris surface roughness
EYE_IrisSaturation_{value} - Iris color saturation
EYE_IrisSpecularity_{value} - Iris specular highlight
EYE_IrisUVRadius_{value} - Iris UV texture radius
EYE_PupilScale_{value} - Pupil size scale
EYE_PupilShiftX_{value} - Pupil horizontal shift
EYE_PupilShiftY_{value} - Pupil vertical shift
EYE_ScaleIrisWithinMask_{value} - Iris scale within mask

Iris Color (12 params) - POST
EYE_AOinIris_{value} - Ambient occlusion in iris
EYE_IrisColor1U_{value} - Primary iris color U coordinate
EYE_IrisColor1V_{value} - Primary iris color V coordinate
EYE_IrisColor2U_{value} - Secondary iris color U coordinate
EYE_IrisColor2V_{value} - Secondary iris color V coordinate
EYE_IrisColorBalance_{value} - Balance between primary and secondary iris colors
EYE_IrisColorBalanceSmoothness_{value} - Color balance blend smoothness
EYE_IrisColorHueVariation_{value} - Iris hue variation amount
EYE_IrisColorValueVariation_{value} - Iris value/brightness variation
EYE_IrisSaturationVariation_{value} - Iris saturation variation
EYE_LimbusDarkAmount_{value} - Dark ring around iris (limbus)
EYE_RadialStructuralBlendSwitch_{value} - Radial vs structural blend mode

Sclera (11 params) - POST
EYE_ScleraCornerDarkHardness_{value} - Corner darkening hardness
EYE_ScleraCornerDarkRadius_{value} - Corner darkening radius
EYE_ScleraFlattenNormal_{value} - Sclera normal map flattening
EYE_ScleraPower_{value} - Sclera brightness/power
EYE_ScleraRotate_{value} - Sclera texture rotation
EYE_ScleraRoughness_{value} - Sclera surface roughness
EYE_ScleraSpecularity_{value} - Sclera specular highlight
EYE_ScleraTintU_{value} - Sclera tint U coordinate
EYE_ScleraTintV_{value} - Sclera tint V coordinate
EYE_VeinsPower_{value} - Blood vein visibility
EYE_VeinsRotate_{value} - Blood vein pattern rotation

43 total eye parameters. Iris color uses UV coordinates on a color lookup texture.
Presets batch-send all EYE_ commands at once.
