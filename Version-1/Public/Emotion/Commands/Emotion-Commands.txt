EMOTION_{Emotion}_{Strength}_{Blend}_{BlendMode} - Sets MetaHuman face emotion and animation blend - POST
Format: EMOTION_{Emotion}_{Strength}_{Blend}_{BlendMode}

Params:
  Emotion    - Emotion name (Neutral, Happy, Sad, Surprised, Fearful, Focused, Disgusted,
               Childish, Tired, Annoyed, Confused, Curious, Embarrassed, Angry, Bored,
               Excited, Relaxed, Suspicious, Proud, Pained, Nervous, Love)
  Strength   - FaceEmotionStrength 0.0-1.0 (intensity of the emotion)
  Blend      - FaceAnimationBlend 0.0-1.0 (mix between default slot and ZenBlink animation)
  BlendMode  - Override (replaces face animation) or BlendByWeight (layers on top)

Defaults: Neutral, 0.8, 0.77, BlendByWeight

Examples:
  EMOTION_Happy_0.8_0.77_BlendByWeight
  EMOTION_Sad_0.5_0.6_Override
  EMOTION_Angry_1.0_0.9_Override
  EMOTION_Love_0.7_0.8_BlendByWeight

Use BlendByWeight when lip-sync is active to layer emotion on top of speech.
Use Override when the avatar is not speaking for full emotion control.
