Animated Post-Processing Effects
Mechanism: 30fps setInterval loop with sin/cos/random math driving post-processing commands.

Pulse (3s loop)
  Commands: CGG_Global_Saturation, BloomInt
  Rhythmic saturation and bloom pulses

Day to Night (10s loop)
  Commands: CG_Temp, EXPO_Comp
  Smooth transition from warm day (4500K) to cool night (8000K)

Cyberpunk Glitch (5s loop)
  Commands: CHROME_Int, BloomInt, CGG_Global_Saturation
  Digital distortion with random chromatic aberration spikes

Dream Sequence (8s loop)
  Commands: BloomInt, GRAIN_GrainInten, CGG_Global_Saturation
  Soft ethereal waves with bloom and grain

Neon Nights (6s loop)
  Commands: CGG_Highlights_Gain, BloomInt
  Vibrant RGB color cycling on highlights with pulsing bloom

Underwater (7s loop)
  Commands: CHROME_Int, CG_Temp, CG_Tint, CGG_Global_Gain
  Blue-green tint with chromatic distortion waves

Fever Dream (5s loop)
  Commands: CG_Temp, CGG_Global_Saturation, GRAIN_GrainInten
  Unsettling chaotic color and temperature shifts

Strobe Effect (2s loop)
  Commands: BloomInt, EXPO_Comp
  Rapid flickering between bright and dark at 10Hz

Color Shift (12s loop)
  Commands: CGG_Global_Gain
  Smooth hue rotation through the spectrum via RGB gain cycling

Breathing (6s loop)
  Commands: EXPO_Comp, VIG, BloomInt
  Slow expansion and contraction of exposure, vignette, and bloom
