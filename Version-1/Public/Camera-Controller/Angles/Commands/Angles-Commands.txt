Camera angle presets (CAMSHOT) - POST
CAMSHOT.Default - Default camera position
CAMSHOT.ExtremeClose - Extreme close-up shot (face detail)
CAMSHOT.Close - Close-up shot (head and shoulders)
CAMSHOT.HighAngle - High angle looking down
CAMSHOT.LowAngle - Low angle looking up
CAMSHOT.Medium - Medium shot (waist up)
CAMSHOT.WideShot - Wide shot (full body and environment)

Uses dot separator. Snaps camera instantly to the predefined position.
