Camera controller for MetaHuman scenes. Supports preset camera angles (CAMSHOT), full 6-axis position/rotation control (CAMSTREAM), animated camera movements at 30fps, and device view mode switching (VIEW).

Angles/ - Preset camera angle commands (CAMSHOT)
Full-Camera-Control/ - Manual 6-axis camera position and rotation (CAMSTREAM)
Animated-Camera/ - Time-based animated camera movements that cycle CAMSHOT/CAMSTREAM commands at 30fps
View-Mode/ - Device aspect ratio switching between mobile (9:16) and desktop (16:9)
