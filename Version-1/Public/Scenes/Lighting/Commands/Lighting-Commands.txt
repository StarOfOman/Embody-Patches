Scene Lighting - POST

Directional Light (Global):
DL_Int_{value} - Directional light intensity (0-10, step 0.01)
DL_Color_{r}_{g}_{b} - Directional light color (r/g/b floats 0-1)

Volume Light (Per-Object, requires spawned LIGHT.Volume):
LIGHTC_{tag}_{r}_{g}_{b} - Set light color by tag (r/g/b floats 0-1)
LIGHTI_{tag}_{value} - Set light intensity by tag (0-100, step 0.1)

Static Presets (for LIGHT.Volume objects):
  Warm Glow - r:1 g:0.7 b:0.4, intensity 5
  Cool Blue - r:0.4 g:0.6 b:1, intensity 4
  Neon Pink - r:1 g:0.2 b:0.8, intensity 8
  Forest Green - r:0.2 g:0.8 b:0.3, intensity 3
  Sunset - r:1 g:0.5 b:0.2, intensity 6
  Moonlight - r:0.7 g:0.8 b:1, intensity 2
  Fire - r:1 g:0.3 b:0.1, intensity 10
  Purple Haze - r:0.6 g:0.2 b:1, intensity 5

Animated Multi-Light Effects (spawn extra LIGHT.Volume objects, animate at 20fps):
  Fireflies (+5 lights) - Blinking yellow-green lights drifting randomly
  Spooky Ghosts (+3 lights) - Slow eerie blue floating lights
